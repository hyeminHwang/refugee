Yeonmi Park's story "In order to live"

Park Yeon-mi is a North Korean defector who escaped to China in 2007 and settled in South Korea.

As her father was sent to a labor camp for smuggling, her family faced starvation.

With the aid of human traffickers, Park and her motehr escaped North Korea by crossing a frozen river and three mountains to get into the Chinese border and by walking through the Gobi desert.

On 1 April 2009, Park and her mother were sent to Ulan Bator’s Chinggis Khaan Airport to fly them to Seoul.
